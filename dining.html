Breeze Restaurant

Breeze is Atmosphere’s light and airy all-day dining restaurant. Seating up to 90 guests, this food venue is a great place to start the day with its spectacular views of the pools, beach and ocean. A continental complimentary breakfast is offered from 7am until 9.30am, with an all-day menu offered all day until 10pm. With an open show kitchen, guests can interact with the chefs whether they are choosing the ingredients for their Asian wok or selecting local snapper for the grill.

Ocean Restaurant

Ocean, our fine dining restaurant overlooks Apo and Siquijor islands. Ocean restaurant will open for groups for Fine Dining in the evening but the Ocean menu is available for all, with a private set-up on the beach or in our tree house (requiring advance reservation of one day). The glass-fronted kitchen allows you to watch our chefs at work, and the adjacent wine tasting room will tempt you in for a tasting session with our wine connoisseur, Combining Filipino favourites and classic international cuisine.

The Poolside Bar

A variety of seating is offered around our well-stocked poolside bar, including high tables for 4 and comfortable sofas on the poolside deck. There is a separate bar snack menu offering finger food and other smaller food items. You are also welcome to order from the all-day dining menu at the bar. Our daily happy hour is between 5 and 6pm, where select drinks are two for the price of one!

The Bakery and Coffee Shop

By the family pool, our bakery and coffee shop serves elegant cakes, fresh cookies and decadent desserts to go with our delicious cafe lattes and other mouth-watering drinks. Through the glass walls, you can also enjoy watching our pastry chefs bake fresh bread each morning. If you wish to take your lunch or dinner at the Bakery, you are welcome. This is the most relaxed of our food venues.

The Treehouse

In our elevated treehouse overlooking the sea, we offer private dinners for up to 10 guests. Alternatively, it offers the perfect romantic setting where you can privately dine with your own waiter, whilst listening to the breeze rustling the leaves of the trees. Let us know if you are celebrating – an anniversary, a birthday or an engagement and we can make your meal extra special!

The Beach

With your toes in the sand and a great view of Apo Island and Siquijor Island, nothing is more tropical than dinner on the beach. We offer beach barbecues regularly for all our guests, and private candle-lit dinners are always special (when the weather permits). Please contact us in advance and we will happily set up an unforgettable dining experience.
