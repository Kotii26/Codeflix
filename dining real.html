Ocean Restaurant

Ocean, our fine dining restaurant overlooks Apo and Siquijor islands. Ocean restaurant will open for groups for Fine Dining in the evening but the Ocean menu is available for all, with a private set-up on the beach or in our tree house (requiring advance reservation of one day). The glass-fronted kitchen allows you to watch our chefs at work, and the adjacent wine tasting room will tempt you in for a tasting session with our wine connoisseur. When dining at Ocean, we ask that you are fully dressed, but formal attire is not expected. Combining Filipino favourites and classic international cuisine to produce a unique fine dining experience, we are certain your taste buds will be dazzled.
